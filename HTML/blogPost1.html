It's Orientation Week

Here's an idea of what lies ahead...


Let's be honest, orientation just wasn't the most exciting thing I had to look forward to...

That said, it was by all means a worthwhile experience, and one thta certainly gave me feel of what the next few weeks were going to be like - it's week 5 and counting, and I wasn't too far off.

I didn't get to interact much, if at all with my soon-to-be module facilitators during orientation week, and was yet to find out what they might be like. Looking back in hindsight, I can say I haven't been disappointed, despite the brief moments here and there that I needed to recalibrate my mind-set as a learner, to suit a more mature way of study - something i've come to rather appreciate.
