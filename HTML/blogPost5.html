We'll cross that bridge when we get there.

An mind-set.

"We'll cross that bridge when we get there" - Though not always the most positive mind-set to have - generally speaking; a very useful one to have, when it comes to learning the tricks of the trade, in the great big world of software engineering - especially as a beginner.

- It's no secret; there's a lot to take in. It can be intimidating and highly over-whelming to try and absorb so much knowledge at one go. So learn to appreciate each challenge as it comes, and not to get held-up on your sticking points.

There's so much more that could be said about it, - but this is only a demo blog, and I'm way over-due on turning it in!
