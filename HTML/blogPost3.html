My Week 3 Experience

Seeing stars; problems or possibilities?

Only the tip of the ice-berg?!

"Wait, CSS and HTML aren't even considered programming languages?!"

Yes, I heard Samson correctly. My excitement to get past the elementary learning and dive straight into the "good-stuff" would soon be short-lived, as I came to the realization that said good stuff - though complex to my seemingly peanut-sized brain, at the time - just wasn't indeed as advanced as I had thought. I was getting quite the bargain already; so you can imagine my amazement the following Monday, when along came JavaScript...but that's a story for another day.

Full disclosure, week 3 had me a little bit in my feelings; with great excitement at the possibilities that lay ahead, as well as a slight urge to pull my hair out eveytime my efforts didn't go according to plan. I can hardly wait for what lies ahead! - I might not like it right away, but I know it will be worthwhile.
